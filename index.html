About

Gabriella Danerlöv is a Stockholm-based fashion designer. She studied at Beckmans College of Design, graduating with a BA in Fashion Design.

She designs and constructs all of her garments herself. Each piece is handmade — cut, sewn and finished in her studio. Her work sits outside mainstream fashion: it is made slowly, with intention, in small numbers or as one-offs.

Alongside her own work, Gabriella does costume design. She has worked as a costume design assistant on the film The Dance Club and on productions for SVT and Netflix.

Custom orders are open. Write to her.
