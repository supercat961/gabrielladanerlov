I make clothes. Not collections, not seasons — just clothes. Each piece is cut and sewn by hand in my studio, from fabrics I choose because I like them, not because they're trending.

Some things are for sale. Others are for rent — for people who want to wear something real for one night without owning it forever. Both feel right to me.

I'm interested in garments that do something — that change the way you hold yourself, or the way a room reads you when you walk in. Not fashion exactly. Just clothing that isn't nothing.

Custom orders are open. I work slowly and I take it seriously. If you have something in mind, write to me.
