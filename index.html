About

Made by hand.
Worn with feeling.

Elena Vasquez · [Your city]

I started making clothes because I couldn't find what I was looking for anywhere else. Not in stores, not in markets, not online. So I learned to sew — first badly, then less so — and somewhere along the way it became the thing I do every day.

My pieces are made in my studio, cut from fabrics I choose slowly and source carefully. Each one is finished by hand. I don't do collections or seasons — I make what I feel like making, and when something is gone, it's gone. Some pieces are available to buy outright; others I rent for occasions where one unforgettable night is enough.

I'm based in [your city]. I welcome custom inquiries, alterations, and long conversations about fabric.
