about

photo here

Gabriella Danerlöv is a Stockholm-based fashion designer and clothing maker. She studied at Beckmans College of Design, graduating with a BA in fashion design.

She designs and constructs all of her garments herself, working from her studio. Her pieces are handmade, made in small numbers or as one-offs, available to buy or rent.

In addition to her own work, Gabriella does costume design. She has worked as a costume design assistant on the film The Dance Club and on productions for SVT and Netflix.

Custom orders are welcome. Write to her.
